Toy Models

View source on GitHub

A small collection of self-contained demo apps, each living in its own directory of this repository. They are designed to aid discussion and build a shared understanding of the key concepts in each domain. Pick one below.

Occupancy & Percentiles

Simulates daily occupancy and shows why p50/p85/p95/p99 give very different absolute answers from the same data.

R in the browser · webR

Flu Season Shapes

Weekly flu patients overlaid across seasons, showing early-peak, late-peak and slow-burn shapes — and which past season a new one resembles so far.

R in the browser · webR

Average vs Intraday Bed Modelling

Why sizing beds on an average (admissions × length of stay) under-provisions versus an hour-by-hour model of arrivals, length of stay and discharge timing.

R in the browser · webR

Population Pyramids — English Geographies

Two population pyramids side by side for chosen areas and years, with change by age band and sex below. Region, sub-ICB or local-authority level, multi-select. Real ONS 2022-based projections.

R in the browser · webR

England Geography Map

Linked list & Leaflet map selector for English regions, sub-ICBs and local authorities — tick the list or click the map, they stay in sync. ONS boundaries.

Leaflet · OpenStreetMap

Waiting Lists: Shape & Simulation

Queueing theory for backlogs (NHS RTT, MRI, endoscopy, letters…): the exponential + targets, how constraining long waiters reshapes the list, and a referral/removal simulator.

R in the browser · webR
