Population Pyramids — English Regions

View source on GitHub

Two population pyramids side by side for a chosen English region, each at a year you pick, with the change by age band and sex between them shown below (absolute and percentage). Data: ONS 2022-based subnational population projections (Regions in England, 5-year age groups, migration variant), mid-2022 to mid-2047. Rendered in R, in your browser, via webR.
