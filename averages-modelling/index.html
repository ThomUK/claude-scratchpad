Average vs Intraday Bed Modelling

View source on GitHub

Bed planning often uses an average: beds = admissions/day × mean length of stay (Little's Law) — a single flat number. But real occupancy swings through the day as admissions arrive before discharges happen, and stacks up at weekends when fewer patients go home. This compares that flat average against an hour-by-hour deterministic flow model over a week. It runs in R, in your browser, via webR.
