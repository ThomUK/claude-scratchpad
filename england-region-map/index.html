A linked geography selector. Pick a level — region, sub-ICB, or local authority — then tick areas in the list to highlight them on the map, or click areas on the map to toggle them in the list; the two stay in sync. Boundaries: ONS (2022 / 2023). Map by Leaflet with OpenStreetMap tiles.
